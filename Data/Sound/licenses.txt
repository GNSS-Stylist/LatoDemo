by Tatiana Lugovaya
https://physionet.org/content/ecgiddb/1.0.0/
License: ODC-By v1.0 (https://physionet.org/content/ecgiddb/view-license/1.0.0/)
Original publication: (https://www.physionet.org/physiobank/database/ecgiddb/biometric.shtml)
Lugovaya T.S. Biometric human identification based on electrocardiogram. [Master's thesis]
Faculty of Computing Technologies and Informatics, Electrotechnical University "LETI",
Saint-Petersburg, Russian Federation; June 2005.
